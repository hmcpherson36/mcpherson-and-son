Our Mission

At McPherson & Son, our mission is to deliver exceptionally creative results to our customers while upholding values of integrity and quality.

About the Owner

Jeff McPherson founded the business with a passion for remodels and repairs instilled in him by his father, Jerry. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, Jeff has built a reputation for personalized service and outstanding results.

A Family Business

McPherson & Son has proudly operated as a family-owned business for over 20 years. Jeff's wife, Christina McPherson shares his passion for redesigns, and often aids in interior design affairs. They also have 3 children, Emma, Holly, and Colin.
